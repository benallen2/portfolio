About Me

I'm Ben Allen, a self-taught web developer living in the Washington, D.C. area. I have been learning web development since June 2016 and have learned HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, and Bootstrap so far. I am currently working on React.js and Node.js.


Before web development consumed my life in the best of ways, I worked on Capitol Hill where I thoroughly destroyed all of my desire to work on policy or politics ever again. Prior to that I was an English teacher in South Korea where I discovered my love for learning new things outweighs my interest in teaching, though I loved living abroad and the travel it allowed me to do.


So far, teaching myself web development has been an amazing journey where the triumphs dwarf the frustrations and I feel empowered to influence the world around me for the better. I am excited every day to move forward and expand my skills.
